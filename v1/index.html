Different Resume Types

Our team of writers have expertise in diverse industries and can curate an extensive range of resumes

Chronological

A chronological resume starts by listing your work history, with the most recent position listed first.

Combination

Highlights both your skills and traits and provide a chronological listing of your work experience.

Mini-Resume

It functions as a document that is somewhere between the business card and the resume.

Functional

It focuses on your skills and experience and de-emphasizes your work history.

Targeted

Customized and specified to the position you want including history, abilities, and education.

Infographic Resume

Such resumes play with graphical elements to present information from top to bottom.
